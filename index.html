About

Hello I'm Awodwa Hendrick, a passionate web developer with a background rooted in the vibrant landscapes of the Eastern Cape, now navigating the digital avenues of Cape Town. My journey into the world of web development began after completing my education at Oval North High School. Eager to dive deeper into the realm of technology, I embarked on a short course in software engineering, where I honed my skills in languages such as Python and Java, and familiarized myself with essential development tools like Git for version control and MySQL database management. While my professional journey is still in its early stages, I am currently immersing myself in a comprehensive 6-month web development course at Life Choices. Here, I am eagerly absorbing knowledge and refining my skills under the guidance of seasoned professionals, preparing to make meaningful contributions to the dynamic field of web development.
